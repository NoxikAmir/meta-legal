Data Deletion Policy

Effective Date: September 25, 2025

1. Overview

Facebook Video Scheduler stores only the minimal data required to operate the service, such as Facebook Page ID, Access Token, and technical logs. No personal user content is permanently stored on our servers.

2. Automatic Data Handling

  • Access tokens are stored locally in environment variables (.env) on your machine.
  • Temporary video files are deleted automatically after processing.
  • Log files (errors/scheduling) are kept only for troubleshooting and can be deleted anytime.
